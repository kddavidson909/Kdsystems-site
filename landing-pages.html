Questions

FAQ

How long does it take to build a site?
Most landing pages are ready for review within 3–5 business days. Larger multi-page sites can take 1–2 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline before we start.
Do you use templates or page builders?
No. Everything is written in clean HTML and CSS from scratch. That means faster load times, no bloated plugins, and code you actually own.
What do I need to provide?
Your logo, the text content for each section, and any photos you want to use. If you don't have copy written yet, I can help with that too. Just mention it when you reach out.
Do you offer hosting?
I use Netlify for hosting, which has a free tier that covers most small business sites comfortably. I set it up and connect your domain. You won't need to touch anything technical.
Can I update the site myself after it's done?
Yes. The files are yours and the code is readable. If you're comfortable editing HTML, you can make changes yourself. If not, I offer small update packages for ongoing tweaks.
