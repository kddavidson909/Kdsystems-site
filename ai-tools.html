Questions

FAQ

What AI tools do you build with?
Primarily the OpenAI API (GPT-4o) and Anthropic's Claude API, depending on the use case. I also work with Make and Zapier's built-in AI actions for lighter-weight automations that don't need a full custom build.
How much does it cost to run an AI tool after it's built?
It depends on volume and which model you're using, but most small business tools run for a few dollars a month. A lead follow-up bot processing 100 leads a month, for example, typically costs under $5 in API fees. I'll give you a clear estimate before we start.
Will I need a developer to maintain it?
For most tools, no. Adjusting the prompts or rules is usually just editing a text document. If something breaks or needs a bigger change, I'm available for ongoing support.
Can AI replace my staff?
That's not really the goal. The tools I build take care of the repetitive stuff: drafting first-pass emails, sorting incoming requests, pulling info from documents. Your team still handles everything that needs a real person.
Is my business data safe when using AI APIs?
OpenAI and Anthropic both offer API tiers where your data is not used for model training. I'll walk you through the privacy settings when we set up your account, and I avoid sending sensitive customer data through AI models unless there's a clear reason and you've explicitly agreed to it.
