Questions

FAQ

What tools do you work with?
Zapier, Make (formerly Integromat), GoHighLevel, Notion, Airtable, Google Workspace, Calendly, Typeform, and most tools with a Zapier integration. If you're using something specific, just ask — chances are I can connect it.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
None. I handle all the setup and configuration. After handoff, you don't need to touch anything — the automations just run in the background.
What does it cost to run automations after they're set up?
Most automation platforms (Zapier, Make) have free tiers that cover small volumes. Depending on how much you automate, you may need a paid plan — typically $20–$50/month. I'll factor this into the plan so there are no surprises.
What if something breaks after you hand it off?
The 30-day check-in is there specifically for this. After that window, I offer maintenance packages for ongoing support if you want a longer safety net.
How long does a typical automation project take?
Simple automations (one or two connected tools) are usually done in 2–4 days. More complex multi-step workflows can take 1–2 weeks depending on the number of systems involved.
