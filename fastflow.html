FAQ

Common Questions

Do you charge extra for nights, weekends, or holidays?
No. Prairie Plumbing charges the same flat rates 24/7, 365 days a year. The price you're quoted at 2pm on a Tuesday is the same price you'd get at 2am on Christmas Eve.
How quickly can you get to my home in an emergency?
We guarantee on-site arrival within 60 minutes for emergency calls within our service area. Most jobs, we arrive faster. We'll give you an ETA when you call.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes — every job starts with a free onsite estimate. A licensed technician will assess the situation, explain exactly what's needed, and give you a firm price before any work begins.
Are your technicians licensed and background-checked?
Every Prairie Plumbing technician is state-licensed, fully insured, and has passed a thorough background check. You'll know who's coming to your home before they arrive.
What areas do you service?
We serve the greater metro area and surrounding suburbs. Call us at (555) 947-2100 and we can confirm coverage for your address in under a minute.
