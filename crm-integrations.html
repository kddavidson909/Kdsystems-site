Questions

FAQ

What CRMs do you work with?
HubSpot, GoHighLevel, Pipedrive, Notion (as a lightweight CRM), Airtable, and most platforms that have a Zapier or API integration. If you're already using something, let me know and I can confirm compatibility before we start.
Is my data safe during a migration?
Yes. I always work from an export of your existing data and verify record counts before and after migration. Nothing is deleted from your old system until you've confirmed the new one looks correct.
What if the tools I use don't have a direct integration?
Most tools integrate through Zapier or Make, which covers the vast majority of business software. For tools without that, there are often API or webhook-based solutions. I'll tell you upfront if something isn't feasible.
How long does an integration project take?
Connecting two or three tools typically takes 3–7 days. Larger setups involving a CRM migration and multiple integrations can take 2–3 weeks. I'll give you a clear estimate before we start.
Do I need to be technical to maintain things after handoff?
No. The documentation I provide is written for non-technical users. If something ever breaks or changes, you can reach out and I'll sort it — or you can hand the documentation to whoever manages your tools.
